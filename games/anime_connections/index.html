How to Play?

  • Match 16 characters into groups of 4 based on their similar attributes.
  • Select 4 cards at a time and click Confirm.
  • If you correctly match all four groups, you win the game.
  • You lose a life for each incorrect selection (♥).
  • If you lose all your lives, the game ends.
  • Click New Game to restart.
  • Press the hint button if you need help.
  • The hint will reset your current selection and automatically select 2 characters from the same group.
  • You can use only one hint per game.
